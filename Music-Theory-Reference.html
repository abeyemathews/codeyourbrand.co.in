What is a Triad?

A triad is a three-note chord consisting of a root, third, and fifth. The quality of the third and fifth determine the type of triad.

Major Triad

Formula: Root + Major 3rd + Perfect 5th

Example in C: C - E - G

C E G

Sound: Happy, stable, resolved
